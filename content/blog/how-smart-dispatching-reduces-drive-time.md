# How Smart Dispatching Reduces Drive Time by 40%

For field service businesses, time spent driving between jobs is time not spent earning revenue. Traditional dispatching methods — phone calls, whiteboards, and gut instinct — leave money on the table every single day.

## The Hidden Cost of Inefficient Dispatching

Most service companies don't realize how much inefficient dispatching costs them. Consider a team of 10 technicians, each spending an average of 2 extra hours per day driving due to poor route planning:

- **20 hours/day** of lost productive time
- **$600/day** in wasted labor (at $30/hour)
- **$156,000/year** in lost revenue potential

That's before factoring in fuel costs, vehicle wear, and the opportunity cost of missed jobs.

## What Is Smart Dispatching?

Smart dispatching uses algorithms to automatically assign the right technician to the right job based on multiple factors:

1. **Geographic proximity** — assign the closest available tech
2. **Skill matching** — ensure the tech has the right certifications
3. **Schedule optimization** — minimize gaps between appointments
4. **Traffic patterns** — account for real-time and historical traffic data
5. **Customer priority** — factor in SLA requirements and customer tier

## Getting Started with Smart Dispatching

Transitioning to smart dispatching doesn't have to be complicated. Here's a practical roadmap:

### Step 1: Audit Your Current Process

Track your technicians' routes for one week. Document start times, drive times between jobs, and idle periods. This baseline data will help you measure improvements.

### Step 2: Digitize Your Schedule

Move from paper or whiteboard scheduling to a digital dispatch board. This is the foundation for any optimization.

### Step 3: Enable GPS Tracking

Real-time location data is essential for smart dispatching to work. Modern GPS solutions are lightweight and respect technician privacy while providing the data needed for optimization.

### Step 4: Implement Automated Dispatch

With digital scheduling and GPS data in place, you can enable automated dispatch rules that optimize assignments in real-time.
